Affordable Dog Walking/Dog Sitting in Arlington, Va.

Hi! I'm Bryan McCarthy and I want to walk your dog!

Does your dog need someone to walk them during the middle of the day? Maybe your schedule suddenly changed? I am here to help. My dog walking services are everything you need them to be: reliable, safe, flexible, and fun for your dog.

As a nearly lifelong Arlington resident, I grew up a dog person. My first dog Jack came from the Vola-Lawson ASPCA. Honestly, he was always my dad's dog just like Annabelle in these pictures. As both of these dogs aged, I have come to realize how important it is to keep your dog active. I look forward to meeting you and yours to build a trusted relationship and keep your dog happy and active during the most boring time of his or her life (while you're away).
