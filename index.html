Affordable Dog Walking/Dog Sitting in Arlington, Va.

Hi! I'm Bryan McCarthy and I want to walk your dog!

Does your dog need a walk in the middle of the day? Maybe your schedule has suddenly changed? I am here to help. My dog walking services are everything you need them to be: safe, reliable, flexible, and fun for your dog.

I'm a lifelong Arlington resident and I grew up to be an avid dog person. My first dog Jack came from the Vola-Lawson ASPCA. As he aged I came to realize how important it is to keep your dog active. I enjoy walking any dogs and specialize in walking senior dogs that may require extra care. I look forward to meeting you and yours to build a trusted relationship and keep your dog happy and active during the most boring time of his or her day (while you're away)!
